METRORAIL RULES AND REGULATIONS

The following points represents the rules of Metrorail that should be followed by passengers a failure to follow these rules shall result in prosecution or fine.Please read through these guidelines with understanding or report to your fellow officer for any assistance.

Rules

    DO:

  • Use headphones:Keep the noise from personal audio and video devices at a level that doesn't disturb other passengers.
  • Board and exit properly:Allow passengers to exit the train befoere you attempt to board.Stand back from the platform edge especially when the train is arriving.
  • Be considerate:Offer your seat to those who need it more,such as senior citizens or people with disabilities.Be mindful of others and thier space.
  • Dispose of waste properly:Use designated bins for trash and recycling.
  • Follow instructions:Pay attention to announcements and instructions from the Metrorail staff or security personnel.
  • Report unattended packages:Alert a transit police officer, station manager if you see any unattended packages.
  • Maitain calm in emergencies:Follow instructions and try to remains calm during emergencies.
  • Mind the gap:Be mindful of the gap between the train door and the platform edge when entering or exiting.

    DON'T DO:

  • Eat,drink,smoke or litter:These actions are not permitted on Metrorail trains or in stations.
  • Obstruct train doors:Do not try to block or force train doors open.
  • Touch train doors:Avoid touching the train doors while they are opening or closing.
  • Lean against train doors:This is a safety hazard and is not allowed.
  • Run in the station:Running is not permitted in the station area.
  • Sit on the platform edgeThis is dangerous and is not permitted.
  • Walk on the trackbed:Walking on the trackbed is strictly prohibited.

3. Passenger Conduct

To ensure safety and comfort for all passengers:

  • No smoking, alcohol consumption, or illegal drug use is permitted on any Metro Rail property.

  • Audio devices must be used with headphones to avoid disturbing others.

4. Schedules and Delays

Metro Rail makes every effort to maintain published schedules. However, delays may occur due to weather, technical issues, or other unforeseen circumstances.

  • Metro Rail is not liable for missed connections, appointments, or any indirect losses due to delays.
  • We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes prior to scheduled departure.

5. Safety and Security

  • All passengers are subject to security screening.
  • Baggage must be within the size and weight limits set by Metro Rail.
  • Items deemed dangerous or prohibited (e.g., explosives, flammable substances) are not allowed on board.
  • Emergency exits must be kept clear at all times.

6. Privacy Policy

7. Refund and Cancellation
